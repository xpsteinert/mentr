# Sick Leave Process

This document describes how to report and handle sick leave at
Example AG.

## Reporting sick

Notify your manager **and** the HR team by 9:00 AM on the first day of
absence, via the "PersonalPilot" app (Absence > Report Sick) or, if the
app is unavailable, by email to hr@example-ag.example. Also inform
your team via the shared team calendar so meetings can be reassigned.

## Doctor's note (medical certificate)

A doctor's note (Arbeitsunfähigkeitsbescheinigung, "AU") is required
starting from the **4th consecutive day** of illness. Upload it as a PDF
or photo in PersonalPilot under Absence > Documents within two working
days of your doctor's visit. If you're unsure whether you need one
earlier — for example, your manager can request one from the first day
in individual cases — ask HR directly.

## Long-term illness

If an absence continues beyond two weeks, HR will reach out to check in
and explain continued pay entitlements. After six weeks of continuous
illness (or repeated related absences within a 12-month period),
statutory sick pay from the health insurance provider takes over from
company sick pay, per German law.

## Returning after extended illness

For absences longer than six weeks, Example AG offers a
structured reintegration process (Betriebliches
Eingliederungsmanagement, "BEM") in coordination with HR and, if
desired, the employee's doctor. This is voluntary, confidential, and
aimed at a gradual, sustainable return — for example a reduced-hours
schedule for the first few weeks. Contact HR at
bem@example-ag.example to start this process.
