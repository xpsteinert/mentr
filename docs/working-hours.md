# Working Hours Model

This document describes the flexible working-time model at Example AG.

## Core hours and flextime

Core hours, during which all employees are expected to be reachable,
are **10:00 AM to 3:00 PM**, Monday to Friday. Outside core hours,
employees may flex their start and end times within the framework of
**6:00 AM to 8:00 PM**, as long as contracted weekly hours are met and
team coverage is maintained.

## Homeoffice policy

Employees may work remotely up to **2 days per week** by default,
coordinated with their team so that core collaboration days are covered
in the office. Fully remote arrangements are possible by individual
agreement with a manager and HR, documented in PersonalPilot under
Profile > Work Arrangement.

## Time tracking

All working time is logged in **PersonalPilot** (Time Tracking > Log
Hours), either via clock-in/clock-out or manual daily entry, by the end
of each working day. Accurate time tracking is a legal requirement
under the Working Hours Act (Arbeitszeitgesetz) and is reviewed monthly
by HR.

## Overtime

Overtime beyond contracted weekly hours should be agreed with your
manager in advance where possible. Recorded overtime is primarily
compensated with time off in lieu, taken within three months; by
exception and with manager approval, it may be paid out according to
the employment contract's terms.

## Break rules

A minimum 30-minute break is required after 6 hours of work, and 45
minutes after 9 hours, per the Working Hours Act. Breaks may be split
into segments of at least 15 minutes each and must be logged in
PersonalPilot like regular working time.
