# Vacation Planning

This document describes vacation entitlement and the request process at
Example AG.

## Entitlement

Full-time employees receive **30 vacation days per calendar year**
(pro-rated for part-time or partial-year employment). Public holidays in
the employee's federal state do not count against this entitlement.

## Requesting time off

Submit vacation requests through PersonalPilot (Absence > Request
Vacation), specifying the date range and, for periods longer than five
consecutive days, a proposed cover/substitution plan. Requests should be
submitted at least **two weeks in advance**; for periods over ten
consecutive days, please submit at least **six weeks in advance**.

## Approval process

Your direct manager approves or declines the request in PersonalPilot,
typically within three working days. Approval considers team coverage
and any overlapping requests — first-submitted requests generally take
priority when the team calendar is tight.

## Cover / substitution

For absences longer than three working days, agree on a named
substitute with your manager before the request is approved, and leave
a short handover note in the team wiki for any open tasks or deadlines
falling within your absence.

## Carry-over and expiry

Unused vacation days may be carried into the following year, but must
be used by **March 31** of that year unless an exceptional business
reason (documented by your manager) applies. Days not used by then are
forfeited, in line with standard German vacation law practice.

## Company-wide shutdown days

Example AG closes company-wide between **December 24 and January
1** each year (exact dates announced by HR every October). These days
are deducted from each employee's vacation entitlement and are blocked
automatically in PersonalPilot — no individual request needed.
